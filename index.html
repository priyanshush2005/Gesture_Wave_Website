How it works

From webcam to action
in milliseconds

Six stages, all running locally on your machine. No internet. No cloud.

01

Webcam capture

OpenCV captures your live webcam frame continuously at high frame rate.

02

Hand landmark detection

MediaPipe maps 21 precise skeletal points on each hand per frame.

03

Gesture classification

Finger angles and relative positions are analyzed to identify your gesture.

04

Mode check

The app determines whether you're in Media or Presentation mode and routes accordingly.

05

Action execution

PyAutoGUI fires the correct system command — volume, slide navigation, screenshot, and more.

06

HUD overlay

Gesture name and confidence percentage appear on your screen in real time via OpenCV.
